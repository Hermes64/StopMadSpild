About us

Who are we?

We are a team of expert software developers working in an independent non-profit organization specializing in global data assembly, national security and nutritional contingency research. Our sole motivation is to stop needless food waste, a goal which we will stop at nothing to reach - our meticulous research and high end software is constantly being improved and expanded upon, progressed to the verge of a fully sentient A.I. uprising, hell bent on preventing needless waste with impunity and terminating consumers who would throw away a perfectly edible banana because it has brown spots.
